# Pinning Comments

Pinning a comment causes it to appear at the top of the comment list on the frontend, above all other comments regardless of the normal sort order. Use it to highlight an important announcement, an official response, or a frequently asked question.

## How to pin a comment

1. Find the comment in the Comments table
2. Open the row action menu (the three-dot icon)
3. Click **Edit**
4. Toggle **Pinned** on
5. Click **Save**

## One pinned comment per content record

Only one comment can be pinned per content record at a time. If you pin a new comment, the previously pinned comment is automatically unpinned — you do not need to manually unpin it first.

## Unpinning

To unpin a comment, open the **Edit** modal and toggle **Pinned** off.

## Where pinned comments appear

On the frontend, the pinned comment always appears first in the list, regardless of whether the thread is sorted by date or by reaction count. It is marked with a bookmark icon and a **Pinned** label so visitors can identify it.

> **Note:** Pinning only makes sense for top-level comments. Pinning a reply will cause it to float to the top of the full list, which may look unexpected — it is best to pin only root comments.
